In what century did the Industrial Revolution take place?
18th (or 19th) century
What was the name of the textile machinery invented by James Hargreaves during the Industrial Revolution?
Spinning Jenny
What was the name of the steam engine developed by James Watt during the Industrial Revolution?
Watt steam engine
What was the name of the social and economic system that emerged during the Industrial Revolution, characterized by the factory system and wage labor?
Capitalism
What was the name of the group of English textile workers who destroyed machinery in protest against the Industrial Revolution in the early 19th century?
Luddites
